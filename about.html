This is a tool to help you visualize and understand the data structures and algorithms covered in the GT CS1332 course. It is based on the Data Structures Visualizations website by David Gales. The CS1332 Visualization Tool was adapted and expanded by Rodrigo Pontes in 2019, under the supervision of Mary Hudachek-Buswell, to include data structures and algorithms covered in the course.
